The Fair Game

How is a worker benefitted?

1)>Get promoted to higher grades | Ensure higher payments | Review HIT design | Get selected jobs as per your own expertise

How is a requester benefitted?

Choose your own grade of workers |Ensure quality by eliminating lower grades | Get accepted work reviewed by other workers | Classify your HIT with tags | Reward Workers with extra pays

Working

What does a worker do?

  1. Enter your username
  2. Click on “See Available Task” button
  3. Task allotted based on your grade and profile
  4. If interested, then choose “Accept” option and click on “Proceed Button”
  5. If not interested, choose on “Reject” option
  6. State reason for rejection in the text box
  7. If you are dissatisfied with the HIT, you may downvote
  8. Finally click on “Proceed” to view next allotted task

What does a requester have to do?

  1. Design the task as per your requirements
  2. Categorize your task using tags from dropdown menu labelled as “Choose from Existing tags”
  3. Choose the grade of workers- this shall eliminate workers of lower grade
  4. Decide a payment as per your work(Keep in mind: minimum pay = minimum wage of selected grade)
  5. Submit Task
